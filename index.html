The vibe

Simple rules.
Maximum freedom.

If you're a decent human who enjoys EVE, you'll fit right in.

01
Real life comes first. No attendance requirements, no guilt trips. Log in when you want.
02
No drama. Respect each other. Disagreements happen — keep them civil.
03
Discord for PvP ops. You don't need to talk, but you need to listen when fleets are active.
04
Help when you can. If someone needs advice or a fleet buddy, be the person who says yes.
05
You don't need to be a pro. Just enjoy the game and want to be part of something.
